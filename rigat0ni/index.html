ABOUT US

Rigatoni is founded by Andrew Bruno in 2017. We are an authentic pasta specified restaurant that provides various pasta that is made by top international chefs.

  • We are the people who are passionate about pasta!
  • All ingredients are prepared fresh every morning.
  • We have a capacity to hold 200+ customers. Large groups are welcome
  • We are a family friendly restaurant.
  • A large see-through playground room is provided for children.
